Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Dj Movies (“we”, “our”, or “the app”) collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application and related services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect and process the following:

  • Account and device: To provide the service we use anonymous sign-in and may associate your use with a device identifier so that one account is linked per device. We do not require your name or email to use the app.
  • Profile (optional): If you choose to set a display name or a phone number for payments, we store that in our systems to provide profile and payment features.
  • Usage and preferences: Watch history, library, cart, download metadata, and in-app preferences so we can sync your experience across sessions and devices where you are signed in.
  • Push notifications: We use Firebase Cloud Messaging. To send you notifications (e.g. movie alerts), we store and use a push token and related data necessary for delivery.
  • Support and feedback: If you contact us or submit a problem report or use in-app chat, we process the content and any identifiers you provide to respond and improve the service.
  • Technical data: We use Firebase Analytics (Google Analytics for Firebase) and related Firebase/Google tooling to collect app version, device type, general usage events, and diagnostics to operate, secure, and improve the app.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information above to:

  • Provide, maintain, and personalize the Dj Movies service (movies, watch history, library, cart, downloads).
  • Process subscriptions and payments (e.g. via M-Pesa) when you provide payment details.
  • Send push notifications you have agreed to (e.g. new movies, rewards).
  • Respond to support requests, problem reports, and chat.
  • Enforce our Terms of Use, prevent abuse, and comply with law.
  • Improve the app, fix issues, and analyze usage in an aggregated way.

3. Data Storage and Third Parties

We use Google Firebase and related Google services to run the app, including Authentication, Cloud Firestore, Cloud Messaging (push notifications), Cloud Functions (server-side operations such as payments), and Firebase Analytics. Information may be stored and processed on servers operated by Google in various locations; processing is subject to Google’s and Firebase’s terms and privacy notices. Payment processing may involve third-party providers (e.g. M-Pesa). If you use Google Cast or similar casting features, Google or device manufacturers may collect or process data under their own policies.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties for marketing.

4. Your Rights and Choices

You may:

  • Use the app with minimal data (e.g. anonymous use without setting a name or phone).
  • Disable push notifications in your device or app settings.
  • Request access to or correction of your data by contacting us (see below).
  • Request deletion of your data, subject to legal and operational requirements (e.g. retention for billing or law).

If you are in a region with specific privacy laws (e.g. GDPR), you may have additional rights such as portability or objection to certain processing; contact us to exercise them.

5. Security

We use industry-standard measures and Firebase security features to protect your data. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure; we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Children

Dj Movies is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have done so, please contact us so we can delete it.

7. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top will change when we do. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy. For material changes we may notify you in the app or by email if we have it.

8. Contact

For privacy-related questions, access or deletion requests, or complaints, contact us through the in-app “Report a problem” or support channel, or at the contact details provided in the app or on our website.
