Privacy Policy

Last updated: 8 April 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Dj Movies (“we”, “our”, or “the app”) collects, uses, stores, and deletes your information when you use our mobile application and related services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect and process the following, depending on how you use the app:

  • Account and device: We use Firebase Authentication and may sign you in anonymously. We associate your account with a stable device identifier (for example Android ID or an app-derived fallback) so we can enforce one primary account per device, sync your profile, and link certain payments to your device for access control.
  • Profile (optional): If you choose to set a display name or phone number (for example for profile or M-Pesa), we store that in our systems.
  • Usage and preferences: Watch history, library, cart, download metadata, likes/dislikes, onboarding preferences, and in-app settings. Some of this may be stored on your device (including local databases) and/or in our backend.
  • Comments: If you post comments on titles, we store your comment text, user identifier, and display name as shown in the app. Comments may be visible to other users in the context of the Service.
  • Payments and access: When you pay via M-Pesa or similar, we process payment-related data needed to confirm your purchase, subscription, DJ pass, XP top-up, or other paid feature. Subscription and some pass data may be stored under records keyed by your device identifier as well as your user account, so we can grant access reliably.
  • Push notifications: We use Firebase Cloud Messaging. We store and use push tokens and related data needed to deliver notifications you opt into.
  • Support and feedback: Problem reports, in-app chat or notification threads, and similar messages you send us, including identifiers needed to respond.
  • Technical and analytics data: We use Firebase Analytics (Google Analytics for Firebase) and related Firebase/Google services for app version, device type, usage events, and diagnostics to operate, secure, and improve the app.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information above to:

  • Provide and personalize the Service (catalog, playback, resume watching, library, cart, downloads, recommendations).
  • Verify and grant access for purchases, subscriptions, DJ passes, XP, and other paid features.
  • Send push notifications you have agreed to.
  • Operate support, moderation, fraud prevention, and abuse detection.
  • Enforce our Terms of Use, comply with law, and meet legal or accounting obligations where applicable.
  • Improve reliability and performance of the app.

3. Data Storage and Third Parties

We use Google Firebase and related Google services, including Authentication, Cloud Firestore, Cloud Functions (for example payment callbacks and account deletion), Cloud Messaging, and Firebase Analytics. Data may be processed on Google infrastructure in various regions, under Google’s and Firebase’s terms and privacy notices.

Payment processing involves Safaricom M-Pesa (or other providers we enable). Those providers process payment data under their own policies.

If you use Google Cast or similar casting features, Google or device manufacturers may process data under their own policies.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties for their marketing.

4. Deleting Your Data (In-App)

You can permanently delete your data from our systems using the app’s Settings:

  • Erase All App History & Data — Removes your Firebase user account and associated server data, then clears local app data on the device. After this, opening the app starts a new session (for example a new anonymous account).
  • Delete Account — Performs the same category of permanent server-side deletion and local wipe for the signed-in user.

Depending on your account, server deletion may include (where applicable): your user profile document and subcollections (for example purchases, cart, watch state, likes/dislikes, notifications and related chat messages), device-scoped payment-related records tied to your device identifier (for example subscriptions or DJ passes stored under that device id), your device binding record, user settings, transactions and certain admin notifications referencing your ids, system alerts tied to your user id, your movie comments, and problem reports tied to your user id. We then remove the Firebase Authentication user so the account cannot be signed in again.

Some information may be retained where law, tax, or fraud prevention requires it, or in aggregated or de-identified form that does not identify you. Backups may persist for a limited time before rotation.

If you cannot use the app, you may still contact us (see below) to request deletion, subject to verification and legal limits.

5. Your Other Rights and Choices

You may:

  • Use much of the app without adding a display name or phone number.
  • Disable push notifications in system or app settings.
  • Request access to or correction of personal data by contacting us.

If you are in a region with specific privacy laws (for example GDPR or similar), you may have additional rights such as portability, restriction, or objection; contact us to exercise them.

6. Security

We use industry-standard measures and Firebase security rules and server-side checks to protect data. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure; we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Children

Dj Movies is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have, contact us and we will take steps to delete it.

8. International Users

If you use the Service from outside the country where our servers or providers operate, your information may be transferred to and processed in other countries where privacy laws may differ.

9. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy. The “Last updated” date will change when we do. Continued use after changes means you accept the updated policy where allowed by law. Material changes may be communicated in the app or by other reasonable means.

10. Contact

For privacy questions, access or correction requests, or complaints, use the in-app Report a problem or support channel, or the contact details shown in the app or on our website.
